Who we are

A family shop with a passion for style

Welcome to Alora Boutique — your neighbourhood fashion destination for ladies and teenage girls. We believe every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and uniquely herself.

We stock a handpicked range of ladies kurtas, dresses, salwar suits, casual wear, and custom-designed outfits for teenage girls. Our collection blends modern trends with timeless elegance.

Every piece in our store is carefully selected to offer the best quality at an honest price. Whether you're dressing for a festival, a casual day out, or a special occasion — we have something just for you.
